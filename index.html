## Rules of Memory Game

- You will start by flipping over one card
- If the next card you flip matches, you get +50 to your score
- These cards then disspear
- If the next card you flip does not match or you select same card twice,
   the cards flip back and your score reduces by 20 or 40 depending on the number of cards left
- The game continues until you match all the cards on the board
